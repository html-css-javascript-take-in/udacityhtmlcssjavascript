Recipie for Tea Making 1 cup tea

  • Take half cup milk and pour it in the vessel.
  • Now take 1 cup water and pour in the same vessel
  • Add two table spoons of sugar and tea powder or leaves in it.
  • Add 1 tea spoon of arjun chaal and tea masala to it.
  • Add some pudhina and shreded ginger to it.
  • VOILA you've made an amazing tea.
